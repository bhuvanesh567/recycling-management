Corporate Profile

iWaste Recycling India Private Limited

iWaste Recycling India Private Limited is a purpose‑driven technology and services company headquartered in Vijayawada, Andhra Pradesh, that transforms how organizations handle electronic waste, modernize classrooms with AI‑first interactive panels, and scale IT teams with reliable manpower outsourcing solutions.

We combine sustainable recycling practices, education‑grade hardware and software, and a people‑centric approach to staffing so our clients get compliant, future‑ready, and cost‑effective outcomes.

What We Do

Core Business Pillars

Delivering compliance, sustainability, and technological modernization under one corporate envelope.

E‑Waste Recycling

We responsibly collect, dismantle, and recycle electronic waste (consumer and enterprise devices) following regulatory compliance and best environmental practices, recovering valuable base metals (copper, lead) and ensuring 100% secure data destruction.

AI‑First Education

We supply, install, and support next-generation classroom hardware featuring AI‑first interactive panels. Combined with adaptive classroom softwares, we help regional schools modernise learning ecosystems dynamically.

IT Manpower Staffing

We scale corporate IT and support departments with vetted, flexible resource deployment. From network technicians to administrators, we deliver high-reliability outsourcing solutions across South India.

Our Mission

To eliminate environmental contamination from electronic waste through state-of-the-art closed-loop processing facility standards, while driving digital infrastructure scaling and employment growth in Tier-II and Tier-III cities across Andhra Pradesh and Telangana.

Our Vision

To establish iWaste Recycling India as the standard-setting benchmark for compliance, environmental resource preservation, and corporate governance in circular B2B services, supporting India's transition to digital equity and environmental stewardship.
